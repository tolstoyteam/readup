## Screen 3 — Subscription Page
> ⚠️ High priority screen.

- Pricing tiers
- List of benefits per plan
- Upgrade / Subscribe CTA button

---

## Screen 4 — Home (Main Page)
> This screen should be visually polished and engaging.

- **Рекомендации** — personalized book suggestions based on selected interests (Netflix-style horizontal scrollable rows)
- **Continue Reading** — resume where the user left off
- **Trending** — most-read books across the platform
- **Categories** — browse by topic
- **Quizzes** — quiz cards based on recently read content

---

## Screen 5 — Book Summary Page
*Core product page.*

- Book title + author name
- Estimated reading time (10–15 min)
- Structured content divided into sections
- Audio player *(planned for later)*
- Save / Like button
- Start Quiz button

---

## Screen 6 — Search & Explore

**Search:**
- Full-text search across all books

**Filters:**
- Category
- Difficulty level
- Reading time

**Search History:**
- Displayed at the bottom of the screen for quick re-access

---

## Screen 7 — Library
*User's personal reading space.*

- Saved books
- Completed books
- Books in progress

---

## Screen 8 — Quiz Page
*Shown after finishing a book summary.*

- 3–5 questions per quiz
- Result screen after completion
- Progress automatically saved

---

## Screen 9 — Profile
> Accessible via a profile icon/avatar in the corner — tapping opens a slide-out panel or dropdown menu.

- Selected interests (editable)
- Reading statistics
- Current streak
- Achievements / Badges
- App settings

---

## Screen 10 — Streak & Progress Page

- Daily streak counter
- Reading goals
- Overall stats and progress charts

---

## Screen 11 — Notifications
> Inspired by Duolingo-style engagement mechanics — friendly, motivating, well-timed.

- Daily reading reminders
- Streak alerts
- New content in interests
- Quiz reminders
- Achievement unlocked notifications
